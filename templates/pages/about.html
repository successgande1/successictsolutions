Our Trade Mark

Our Story

SUCCESSGANDE ICT RESOURCES LTD is a dynamic and innovative ICT company that started its operations in Gboko, Benue State, Nigeria, in 2021 under the name SUCCESSGANDE ICT SOLUTIONS. With a passion for technology and a vision to make a positive impact on the Nigerian society, the company has grown and evolved into a private Limited company, registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in Abuja with Registration Number RC:7070522.

Our success story is incomplete without our dedicated and talented team of professionals. Combining expertise across various disciplines, we work collaboratively to deliver holistic and impactful ICT solutions.

We are Certified Web Developers from Google, through the Google African Developer Scholarship Training Program, and we are always and currently undergoing trainings from recognized IT Training firms in emerging programming technologies in order to continue to serve you better.

Our Future:

As we continue to evolve and grow, we remain steadfast in our vision of becoming a leading catalyst for positive transformation in Nigeria's digital landscape. We aim to forge new partnerships, expand our reach, and contribute to the advancement of technology adoption and digital literacy in the country.

Our Services:

At SUCCESSGANDE ICT RESOURCES LTD, we offer a comprehensive range of cutting-edge ICT services to cater to the diverse needs of individuals and businesses alike. Our core services include:

Internet of Things (IoT): Pioneering IoT solutions that seamlessly connect devices and systems, enabling smart, data-driven decision-making.

Programming Training: Empowering individuals with comprehensive computer literacy and web programming training to equip them with the skills needed for a digital future.

ICT Consultancy: Providing expert guidance and strategic insights to assist businesses in leveraging technology for enhanced efficiency and growth.
